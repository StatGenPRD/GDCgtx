Objective(s)

The pharmacogenetic (PGx) study utilized data from this clinical study and genome-wide germline genetic data to test for association between genetic variants and differential efficacy response to [SPECIFY NAMES].

Summary

Of 20 subjects enrolled in the clinical study, a total of 19 (95%) subjects provided PGx consent, a sample for genetic analysis and were successfully genotyped. For PGx analyses, overlapping treatment group(s) were defined as listed in Table 1. Genotype-by-treatment interaction effects were estimated using contrasts between main effects estimated independently for each treatment group.
